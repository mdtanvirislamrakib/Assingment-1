Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh thrives with innovations in agriculture, technology, renewable energy, and startups, driving sustainable economic growth.

Sustainability

Bangladesh embraces sustainability through renewable energy, climate resilience, green agriculture, and community-focused development initiatives.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure is rapidly developing, focusing on transportation, energy, communication, and urbanization, driving economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh thrives through education, innovation, women's empowerment, economic growth, sustainability, and unity, fostering resilient development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future of green energy.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Mohammad Yunus, a Bangladeshi social entrepreneur and economist, revolutionized poverty alleviation through microfinance. Founder of Grameen Bank, he empowered millions, especially women, with small loans to foster entrepreneurship. Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006, Yunus remains a global icon for financial inclusion, social business, and sustainable development in Bangladesh.
